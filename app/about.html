About us

Insight NewYork is a comprehensive tool which enables you to analyze bike and cab data within New York.The insight map provides users with data visualization of cabs and bikes. You can enter your location in New York and check for the density of cabs or bikes within that area. You can use our custom filters to check historical data for the areas you are interested in.
The data provided in analysed form is easy to understand and can be used to make various kind of predictions, depending upon your requirements. For example, you are searching for a house to rent and want to understand the ease of finding a cab,just check our data analytics which provide you all the statistics required for finding out the cab density within the given area or multiple areas. The data analytics provided by insight map gives a comprehensive outlook of cab and bike flows within an area for a certain range of time. Looking at this data it is easy to understand the flow of traffic and various other factors which needs to be considered by an individual before making decisions relating to real estate, housing etc.
